A little more about us.

Just another job site?


We asked this question too. Do we really want to build just another job site. If you have spent some time on the Indonesian job hunt circuit - the answer would be an irrevocable yes.

Employers in indonesia have to go through a complicated process just to get a posting up. Their limited options includes slow premium sites like jobsdb and jobstreet that takes a while to set up. Or low trust, low quality sites with unmanaged content like lowongankerja.co.id. At Jobsy, we understand the problems of 'the average company' and we're providing a low priced- trustworthy solution for elegant job posting.

Jobsy is job board built from the ground up to cater to the modern day companies - we have decided to scrap the complicated processes that are rampant in existing solutions, and provide a clean, easy to use site.

Race with the giants.

At Jobsy, all companies are created equal. There are no premium posts, no featured companies. No matter the size of your company, your post will get equal exposure as the giants. Get a chance to recruit the best possible candidates for a reasonable price.

Manage your recruitment process.

Jobsy will keep track of the number of views, clickthroughs, and applications so you can better understand what is going on with your post. You may adjust, modify, pause your post anytime you like. We will also store all applications for easy access.
